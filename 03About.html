About Us

We are a team of young professionals from the fields of medical science, finance and education who are passionate of exploring places and sharing those experiences with people through this media. We believe that discoverubf the world is liberating to the soul. It broadens everyone's perspective and allows us to be more part of the populace. More than the places that we get to visit are the people that we meet along the journey. We get to understand their culture and traditions. With such, a deeper understanding with another human is born. A form of respect that transcends any other as it creates ties between strangers from different walks of life.

Mission

We are individuals committed to provide insights and ideas about places and sites of wonders to both beginners and veteran travellers. We, therefore, put much effort in sharing honest and authentic experiences and to capture photos of proof to illustrate further what we have written.

Vision

Our team envisions ourselves to be site of choice for travels of different levels of experiences and ages. Further, we see ourselves as promoters of travel lifestyle as our team believes that the world is a giant library that can teach us beyond what classrooms can and for we further know that experience is the mentor for men.
